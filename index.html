Introduction

Welcome to Babylook ("we," "our," or "us"). Babylook is an AI-powered mobile application that generates predictions of what a baby might look like based on parent photos. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Babylook, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

2

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

Photos You Upload

  • Parent photos that you voluntarily upload for baby face prediction
  • These photos are sent to our secure servers solely for AI processing
  • Photos are automatically deleted from our servers after processing is complete

Generated Images

  • AI-generated baby prediction images are stored locally on your device
  • You have full control over these images and can delete them at any time

Usage Data

  • App usage analytics (e.g., feature usage, session duration)
  • Device information (e.g., device model, OS version)
  • Crash reports and performance data

Subscription Information

  • Purchase and subscription status managed through Apple's App Store
  • We do not collect or store your payment information directly
3

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • AI Processing: To generate baby face predictions using our AI models
  • Service Improvement: To improve and optimize our AI algorithms and app performance
  • Subscription Management: To manage your subscription status and provide access to premium features
  • Rate Limiting: To ensure fair usage of our services across all users
  • Technical Support: To diagnose and fix technical issues
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
