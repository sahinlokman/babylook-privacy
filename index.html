üìä Information We Collect

Babylook is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize data collection to only what is strictly necessary for the app to function:

  • We do not collect usage analytics or tracking data
  • We do not collect device information
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect any personal identifiers
  • Photos are transmitted to our AI server solely for portrait generation and are not permanently stored
  • Generated portraits are stored locally on your device for up to 30 days, then automatically deleted

ü§ñ Image Generation and AI Processing

Babylook uses artificial intelligence (AI) to generate baby face predictions. Here is exactly how your photos are processed:

How It Works

  • Photo Transmission: When you tap "Create Baby Portrait," the two parent photos you selected are sent to our external AI processing server for analysis and image generation.
  • AI Model: Our server uses an AI model to analyze facial features, skin tones, and proportions from both parent photos and generate a predicted baby portrait.
  • Data Format: Photos are compressed and encoded before transmission to minimize data usage.

Data Handling During Processing

  • Temporary Processing Only: Your photos are used solely for the purpose of generating the baby portrait. They are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers.
  • No Training Use: Your photos are never used to train, improve, or develop AI models.
  • Automatic Deletion: After the baby portrait is generated and delivered to your device, the parent photos and generated result are automatically removed from the processing server.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: Your photos are not shared with, sold to, or accessed by any third parties beyond what is necessary for image generation.
