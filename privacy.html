üë§ Face Data Collection & Usage

Because Babylook uses parent photos that may contain facial data, we want to be fully transparent about how this sensitive data is handled:

What Face Data We Collect

We collect the photos of the mother and father that you explicitly upload into the app. These photos may contain facial features (face data) of the individuals depicted.

How Face Data Is Collected

Face data is collected exclusively through your deliberate action of selecting photos from your device's photo library within the app. We do not capture or access your camera, and we do not access any photos beyond those you explicitly select.

All Uses of Face Data

  • Portrait Generation Only: The sole purpose of collecting face data is to generate a fictional, AI-created image of what a baby might look like based on the facial traits of both parents.
  • No Identification or Tracking: Face data is never used for biometric identification, facial recognition, user profiling, or tracking of any kind.
  • No AI Training: Your photos and face data are never used to train, improve, fine-tune, or develop any AI models ‚Äî by us or any third party.
  • No Secondary Use: Face data is not used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than generating the portrait you requested.

User Consent

Before any face data leaves your device, the app displays an explicit consent dialog that identifies what data will be sent, who it is sent to (CreativeLabs via Cloudflare Workers), and asks for your permission. You may decline at any time, in which case no data is transmitted. You can also revoke consent at any time from Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Consent.

ü§ñ Image Generation and AI Processing

Babylook uses artificial intelligence (AI) to generate baby face predictions. Here is exactly how your photos are processed:

How It Works

  • Photo Transmission: When you tap "Create Baby Portrait" and consent to processing, the two parent photos you selected are securely sent through Cloudflare Workers (proxy/CDN) to CreativeLabs (AI image generation provider) for analysis and image generation.
  • AI Model: CreativeLabs uses an AI model (Nanobana Pro) to analyze facial features, skin tones, and proportions from both parent photos and generate a predicted baby portrait.
  • Data Format: Photos are compressed and encoded before transmission to minimize data usage.
  • Additional Data: Along with your photos, a text prompt and an anonymous installation identifier (for rate limiting only) are also transmitted.

Data Handling During Processing

  • Temporary Processing Only: Your photos are used solely for the purpose of generating the baby portrait. They are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers or the provider's servers.
  • No Training Use: Your photos and face data are never used to train, improve, or develop AI models.
  • Automatic Deletion: After the baby portrait is generated and delivered to your device, the parent photos and generated result are automatically and immediately removed from the processing server.
  • Retention Period: Face data (parent photos) is retained only for the duration of the single generation request ‚Äî typically a matter of seconds to minutes ‚Äî and is permanently deleted immediately upon delivery of the result. No face data is retained beyond this processing window.
